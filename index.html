Development of Archery as a sport

Archery as a sport was first introduced in 1900 at paris Olympics, was contested again in 1904, 1908 and 1920, then again, after an absence of 52 year it resumed in 1972 at munich. Archery is the first sport to be introduced for both men and women. In India 1 st Archery national was organized by AAI ( Archery Association of India) at new delhi in 1973. In the year 2012 , organized by the HU archery mission & TAAT ( The Archery Association of Tamilnadu) Tamilnadu one of the southern state hosted the first archery nationals among Southern states.

Development of archery in Tamilnadu

Mr Shihan Hussaini ( younger son of prof SAQ hussaini a noted Historian, author & martial artist from Madurai , Tamilnadu ) plays a vital role in development of archery as a sport in Tamilnadu. He is Tamilnadu’s Ever & only level 3 coach certified by WA(World Archery) and has introduced Recurve & compound bow in Tamilnadu. In 1977 Shihan Hussaini started 1 st ever formal Target archery classes at Madurai in Tamilnadu, Shihan Hussaini had travelled various parts of World to improvise the art of archery. Quick archery center in London, Mr bill from Singapore are some among them. In 1993 Shihain Hussaini started Hu Archery mission .In 1998 TAAT( The Archery Association of Tamilnadu) was officially registered as a society under Shihan Hussaini as founder & general secretary and Chandrasekaran as president. TAAT ( The Archery Association of Tamilnadu) got recognition by Tamilnadu Olympic Association (TNOA) in 2007. AAI (Archery Association of India) gave TAAT provisional affiliation in 2010 &amp; confirmed it in 2012. TAAT has 32 districts association registered
under its wing.

ABOUT THE ARCHERY ASSOCIATION OF DINDIGUL DISTRICT

Inaugurated in year 2009. THE ARCHERY ASSOCIATION OF DINDIGUL DISTRICT-TAAD, is affiliated to the archery association of Tamilnadu-TAAT,Tamilnadu state Olympic association-TNOA , sports development Authority of Tamilnadu-SDAT.

The officer bearers of TAAD are P. Jothimurugan (President), P. Seetharaman (Secretary &amp; coach),  R. Madurai veeran (Treasurer ),  Swamynathan (Senior vice president) , Vice presidents: K.B. Saravanan, Rajeshkannan. E c members: Mr. Vignesh, Mr. Manimaran, Mr. Vivek, Mr. Veera kumar, Mr. Rajiv Gandhi.


Mr p.seetharaman secretary of TAAD is coaching the Archers of Dindigul District. He had completed Level one coaching training from TAAT. He had received awards including, District level best physical education teacher ,Dhuronacharya Awards (Maignana sabai) , Best Secretary Award .

Archers under his training had achieved success at various levels.

Oath of the coach

“ I Thank you for putting your trust in me & accepting me as your mind coach & guru for life. I shall strive to be an ideal & inspiring role model & continuously motivate you by example of my own thoughts, words & action. I will push you to identify your strengths & weakness & transform all your negatives in to positives & bring out the best in you. I will constantly care for you & your well being, provoke you to dream big, aim higher & reach every target that you would set your mind’s arrow to. I will be strict in maintaining discipline , forgiving with unintentional mistakes, impartial in my attention, selfless in my motives, generous in my appreciation, constructive in my criticism & humane in correctional punishments. Training Oath

Shooting line Oath
